    Nadia Murillo more known as VOX ANON is a Berlin based Artist and Blogger, currently studying at Digital Career Institute Berlin. Her work focuses on producing fine art prints of her original artworks , web­sites, as well as 3D environments. Collaborations include: Crate and Barrel, Lindstrom, Ikono TV, Soft Gallery, Glossy Tv, Gestalten, Volume Magazine, Equadorian Embassy and Fundacion Guayasamin. Vox’s work has been exhibited worldwide including at the Museum of Surgical Science in Chigago, Nucleus Gallery (LA), Design Walk (Athens), The Dumbo Art Center (NYC), Stroke Festival (München) and more.
